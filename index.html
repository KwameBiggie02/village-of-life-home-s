About Us

Village of Life Children’s Home is dedicated to providing shelter, education, and healthcare to orphaned children.
Our mission is to give them love, hope, and opportunities for a better future.
Founded in 2010, we have served a lot of children, ensuring they receive food, quality education, and emotional support.
Our vision is to see every child reach their full potential and become contributing members of society.

CHILDREN ACTIVITIES

Our children enjoy a wide range of activities such as studying, playing football, drawing, singing, and helping with daily tasks. These activities help them grow, learn new skills, and build happy memories together.
